큐(queue):FIFO(First-in First-out)규칙순서 알고리즘

- 삽입 `enqueue`
- 삭제 `dequeue`

------------------
| 5 | -2 | 10 |
------------------

```py
enqueue(5)
enqueue(-2)
enqueue(10)
dequeue() # -5
dequeue() # -2
```
